Our Story


Sometimes you get tired of listening to the same thing on the radio or maybe your playlists are getting old.

Searching for new music can be time consuming so we created Crate Digger.

Crate Digger is a site made for people looking for new music based off of the artist they already love.

The music lover can search an artist they already love into the Spotify search box. After hitting submit Spotify will find a new random artist based off of the artist you have entered.

If you like the artist, then Ticket Master will find tour dates to see your new favorite band play.

Happy listening!
